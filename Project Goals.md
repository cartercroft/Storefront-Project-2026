Role: User
\[] Users can create an account and login

\[] Users can manage their account information
\[] Users can filter and search items
\[] Users can add/remove products from their cart
\[] Users can edit item quantity in their cart
\[] Users can check out their cart

Role: Seller
\[] Seller users can list and manage their items

\[] Seller can manage their account and company information
\[] Seller users can set the status and availability of their own items

Role: Admin
\[] Admin users can see all products and their owners
\[] Admin users can manage non-admin users and their permissions
\[] Admin users can manage Seller users' items
\[] Admin users can list and manage items
